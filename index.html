About Us

Lady Sally

We believe the caregiver trained in early childhood development and care, as well as basic health and safety skills, generally does a better job with child care. Thus, our goal is to make the early years experience better and more enjoyable for all children, regardless of their background or circumstances, by supporting ALL adults and structures that are engaged with their learning and development.
